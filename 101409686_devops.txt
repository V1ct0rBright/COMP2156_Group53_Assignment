In this course, students will dive into DevOps best practices and tools to boost productivity in tech environments.
They'll explore key developer operation frameworks and industry-standard tools, including Ansible and Git, through hands-on exercises. 
The curriculum emphasizes solving complex business problems within the core responsibilities of a DevOps engineer. 
Students will also learn about crucial data formats such as XML, JSON, YAML, and YANG, highlighting their importance in network automation. 
This course aims to equip students with the skills to implement efficient DevOps solutions..
